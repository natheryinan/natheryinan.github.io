## Coordinated Disclosure Policy

We support responsible security research. Public disclosure should only occur **after**:

1. A fix or mitigation has been released, **or**
2. A mutually agreed-upon disclosure date is reached

Reports submitted in good faith will **never be subject to legal action**.
